The story

The story begins when a young student decided that he has had enough of his daily routine , and since day dreaming always helped , he escaped to his own virtual world where only imagination has the power to rule. He finds himself trapped in an empty cube where he realises that his brain is the main tool for survival since each cube is full of enigmas and surprises and he has to be smart and careful to be able to go from a cube to another without getting hurt or injured by accomplishing severel tasks and discovering a world full of riddles and facts . His world of imagination will lead him into harder tasks to fullfill different challenges until he achieves an absolute goal
